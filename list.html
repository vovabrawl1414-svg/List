Epic Enchantments

Web
Mines entire veins of ore at once. Great for quickly collecting all ores in a mining area.
Magnet
Automatically pulls dropped items into your inventory. Never lose items again while mining or fighting.
Timber
Fells entire trees instantly. Perfect for fast wood collection without chopping each log individually.
Auto-Smelt
Automatically smelts mined ores, saving time and resources by giving you ready-to-use ingots.
Experience
Grants bonus experience from mining, killing, or other actions. Level up faster than usual.
Impenetrable
Makes armor or items harder to damage. Reduces the amount of damage you take.
Vampirism
Heals you when attacking enemies. Great for survival in combat without potions.
Filter
Sorts or filters items automatically in your inventory. Keeps things organized effortlessly.
Excavator
Mines blocks faster in a wider area, like a mini-drill effect for digging large surfaces.
Replanter
Automatically replants crops after harvesting. Perfect for farming efficiently.
Wings of God
Grants protection based on your other armor pieces. The more armor you wear, the stronger the effect.
